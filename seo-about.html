How it all started

The decision to start Ru'ia stemmed from a personal desire for more fulfilling work, especially after my son's arrival inspired me to harness my expertise more impactfully. The 8-5 structure no longer fits my aspirations. Eager to escape bureaucratic monotony, I founded Ru'ia to transform consultancy by streamlining processes and cutting red tape, providing visionary, innovative solutions that benefit all.

Vision

Ru’ia suggests a harmonious vision and an active pursuit to inclusivity and innovation, emphasizing the blending of diverse elements for positive change. It embodies responsiveness, unbiasedness, inclusion, and accessibility, portraying a commitment to creating an environment where gender equality and social cohesion are addressed with a landscape of endless solutions. The name was inspired by the vision it represents and by looking into my son's eyes, who motivates me to be a better version of myself.
