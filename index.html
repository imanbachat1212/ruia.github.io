About

With over a decade of hands-on experience in gender, disability, and diversity inclusion project design, implementation, monitoring, evaluation, and learning, I have a proven track record of managing adaptable projects for several international and local organizations. This includes engaging collaboratively with multiple donors, delivering high-quality reports under tight timelines, and thriving under pressure. I have trained and coached over 1,500 staff by developing personalized plans to maximize their potential. My seasoned background encompasses work in both crisis/ emergency settings and development/sustainable projects, advancing gender equality, women’s empowerment, child protection, youth development, accessibility, diversity inclusion and social cohesion. My expertise lies in analyzing issues through both contextual and intersectional lenses, integrating the principles of responsiveness, impartiality, inclusion, and accessibility into every aspect of my work. I am committed to exchanging knowledge and driving positive change.
